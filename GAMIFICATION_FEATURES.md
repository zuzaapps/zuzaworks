## 🎯 **Gamification Features Implemented**

### **1. Points & Levels System**
- **ZuZa Coins**: Virtual currency earned through actions
- **Experience Points**: Track progress toward next level
- **Level System**: 1-20 levels with increasing difficulty
- **Rank System**: Global ranking among all employees

**Point Awards:**
- Complete shift: 50 points
- On-time arrival: 25 points
- Help colleague: 30 points
- Complete training: 100 points
- Social engagement: 10-50 points
- Perfect week: 200 points

### **2. Streak System** 🔥
- **Login Streaks**: Consecutive days logged in
- **Shift Completion Streaks**: Perfect attendance
- **Training Streaks**: Continuous learning
- **Visual**: Animated fire icon with day count
- **Rewards**: Bonus multipliers for long streaks (7, 14, 30, 60, 100 days)

### **3. Badges & Achievements** 🏆
**Badge Categories:**
- **Consistency**: "Early Bird", "Night Owl", "Weekendwarrior"
- **Performance**: "Speed Demon", "Perfect Week", "Quality Champion"
- **Social**: "Team Player", "Mentor", "Knowledge Sharer"
- **Learning**: "Quick Learner", "Certification Master", "SETA Star"
- **Special**: "Proudly SA", "B-BBEE Champion", "BCEA Guru"

**Badge Display:**
- Animated pop-up when unlocked
- Badge showcase on profile
- Leaderboard badge integration
- Rarity levels (Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary)

### **4. Leaderboard System** 📊
**Multiple Leaderboards:**
- **Points**: Overall ZuZa Coins
- **Shifts**: Most shifts completed
- **Training**: Most training hours
- **Social**: Highest engagement
- **Department**: Within-team competition
- **Location**: Inter-branch competition

**Leaderboard Features:**
- Top 3 highlighted with Gold/Silver/Bronze
- Trend indicators (↑ up, ↓ down, — stable)
- Weekly reset for fresh competition
- All-time hall of fame

### **5. Digital Twin Avatar** 🤖
**Personality Features:**
- Animated robot avatar (bottom-right corner)
- Contextual messages based on user activity
- Proactive suggestions and reminders
- Celebratory messages for achievements
- Coaching for improvement areas

**Popup Triggers:**
- New badge unlocked
- Streak milestone reached
- Shift reminder (30 min before)
- Training recommendation
- Congratulations on performance
- BCEA compliance alerts

**Digital Twin Behaviors:**
- **Speaking animation** when active
- **Hover effects** for interaction
- **Smart timing** (not annoying)
- **Personalized messages** based on user data
- **Emoji usage** for friendly tone

### **6. FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) Elements** ⚡
**Social Proof Indicators:**
- "5 people viewing this shift"

**Live Activity Feed:**

**Urgency Indicators:**
